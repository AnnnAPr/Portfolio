About me

I was the tech enthusiast from my childhood. I liked to disassembled toys to see how they look inside and how they work. Later in my life my interests were changed. My last passion was in cake decoration. Going through a lot of websites about cake decoration, I realized some of websites I liked and some of them I did not. Thoughts how I would share information about my cakes, how would my page looked like, what logic I would use and great desire to build my first website brought me to Generation junior full stack developer program.
